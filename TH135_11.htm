Reimu defeats herself[edit]

Reimu

あんた誰？

Who are you?

Reimu

まさか化け狸？

Don't tell me you're a tanuki.

Reimu defeats Marisa[edit]

Reimu

あんた、宗教家でも
なんでもないでしょ？

You don't even
care about religion, do you?

Reimu

妖怪退治も私に任せてよ

Leave the youkai extermination to me.

Reimu defeats Ichirin[edit]

Reimu

そっちのオヤジさんも
何か言ったらどう？

How about letting the
old man there have a say?

Reimu

妖怪にも仏教にも
負ける訳にいかない！

I can't possibly lose
to youkai or Buddhism!

Reimu defeats Byakuren[edit]

Reimu

お酒を呑まない仏教に負ける
訳にはいかないわ

No way I can lose to Buddhism.
You don't allow alcohol.

Reimu

その巻物……
何て書いてあるの？

What's written on that
scroll of yours?

Reimu defeats Futo[edit]

Reimu

お皿を無駄にしないの！

Don't waste plates!

Reimu

聞いたわよ

あんたのご先祖様って邇藝速日命 (ニギハヤヒノミコト)
なんですって？

From what I hear.

Apparently you're descended
from Nigihayahi no Mikoto?[1]

Reimu defeats Miko[edit]

Reimu

そんな自分勝手な
宗教には負けない！

I won't lose to
such a selfish religion!

Reimu

ところでさ……

やっぱ変だよね
観客の様子

何か聞いてない？

By the way...

The audience is definitely
acting weird.

Have you heard anything?

Reimu defeats Nitori[edit]

Reimu

お祭り騒ぎに便乗して
あくどい商売してるのね

You were using the festival to
price-gouge, weren't you?

Reimu defeats Koishi[edit]

Reimu

あれ？
誰と闘ってたんだっけ？

Huh?
Who did I fight just now?

Reimu defeats Mamizou[edit]

Reimu

化けの皮が剥がれたわね！

I've peeled off your disguise!

Reimu

私に化けるのやめてくれない？
似てないし……

Can you stop transforming into me?
It's a terrible imitation, anyway...

Reimu defeats Kokoro[edit]

Reimu

あんたのお面、怖いよ

Your masks are really freaky.

Reimu

結局見つかったの？

本物の希望のお面
どんな顔してるのかしら？

You have finally found it?

I wonder what kind of expression
your real Mask of Hope even has.

Generic victory[edit]

Reimu

やっぱり神々の力が最強ね

The power of the gods wins out in the end.

Reimu

これで参拝客が増えるといいな

It would be great if I got some more worshipers from this.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A Japanese god who descended into Japan and became the legendary ancestor of the Mononobe clan.
