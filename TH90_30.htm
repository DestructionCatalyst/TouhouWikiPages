Eiki defeats Reimu[edit]

Eiki

妖怪を退治すること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Exterminating Youkai.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Marisa[edit]

Eiki

貴方のため込んだガラクタを土に還すこと。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Returning those sundries you gathered back to the earth.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Sakuya[edit]

Eiki

人間との繋がりを強くもつこと。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Having strong link with humans.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Youmu[edit]

Eiki

貴方のお嬢様の言葉を毎日聞くこと。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Listening to your mistress's words every day.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Reisen[edit]

Eiki

丸い物を集め、それをお供えすること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Gathering round things and offering them.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Cirno[edit]

Eiki

様々な所へ出かけ、世間を知ること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Going to different places and knowing the world.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Lyrica[edit]

Eiki

目上の方を尊敬すること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Respecting your elders.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Merlin[edit]

Eiki

もっと墓場で演奏する回数を増やすこと。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Performing more in the graveyard.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Lunasa[edit]

Eiki

妹達の音を上手くコントロールすること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Controlling your sisters' sounds well.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Mystia[edit]

Eiki

自分の歌の力を理解すること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Understanding the power of your own song.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Tewi[edit]

Eiki

貴方の家族を大切にすること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Taking care of your family.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Aya[edit]

Eiki

積極的に良いニュースを取り上げること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Actively picking up good news.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Medicine[edit]

Eiki

人間に対する憎しみの念を消すこと。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Losing the hatred toward humans.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Yuuka[edit]

Eiki

もっと人間に恐怖を与えること。
これが今の貴方が積める善行よ。

Terrorizing humans more.
This is the good deed you can do right now.

Eiki defeats Komachi[edit]

Eiki

あ、こら待ちなさい！
こんな所で遊んでる場合じゃないでしょ？

Hey, wait a minute!
You don't have time to play around here!

Eiki defeats herself[edit]

Eiki

は～あ。暇ねぇ……
こんなに幽霊が多いのにねぇ……

Huh, so bored...
With all these ghosts around, too...

Eiki defeats someone[edit]

Eiki

少しは善行を積んでいるかしら？

So, did you do some good deeds?

Eiki

貴方達みたいに素直ならやりやすいん
ですけどね。裁判も。

裁判は、お金と法律の知識でどうとでもなる
もの、と勘違いしている輩が多くて多くて。

何時から人間は人間を裁けるようになったの
かしら？　思い上がりも甚だしいわ。

If only it was as honest as you people, trial would be so much easier.

So many people are mistakenly believing that trial can be easily done by knowledge of law and money.

When did humans became able to judge other humans, anyway? So much conceit.
