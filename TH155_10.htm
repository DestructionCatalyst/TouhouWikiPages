Stage 1[edit]

「紫は最後に動く」

"Yukari Takes the Last Move"[1]

BGM:開演間近

BGM:The Curtain Shall Rise Soon

依神女苑
最凶最悪の双子の妹

Joon Yorigami
A Cursed and Evil Younger Twin Sister

Joon

今夜もライブ、楽しみねー！
観客達から富を奪い放題よー

I'm looking forward to tonight's concert too!
We'll take as much as we want from this audience!

依神紫苑
最凶最悪の双子の姉

Shion Yorigami
A Cursed and Evil Older Twin Sister

Shion

ねえ女苑
そろそろ私にもお金を分けてよー

Hey, Joon, isn't it about time
you gave me my cut?

Joon

えー、姉さんに渡すと
すぐに無くしちゃうじゃん
お金は私に管理させてー

What? But if I gave it to you,
you'd just lose it right away.
Let me handle the cash.

Shion

女苑はすぐに使っちゃうんだもん
全然私に回って来ないじゃない

But you just spend it all right away!
Either way it never gets to me!

Joon

使い切れないくらい
奪えば良いのよ
金脈はこんなにあるんだもん

If we run out then we just take more.
That's what it means to have
a fountain of wealth.

Shion

まあそうね
ガッポガッポ稼ごうね

Well, I guess.
Let's rake it in tonight.

Joon

あ、誰か来たわ

Ah, someone showed up.

Yukari

そこまでよ

貧乏神の紫苑に、疫病神の女苑
少し、おいたが過ぎたようね

That's far enough.

Poverty god Shion and plague god Joon,
this mischief has gone on a little too long.

Joon

何なのよ？
もうすぐライブが始まるから
邪魔しないでよね

What's with you?
The concert's about to start,
so stay out of our way.

Yukari

うふふ、嬉しいわ
やっとお前達を懲らしめる時が
来たんだから

Hee hee. I'm so giddy.
I finally get to punish you two.

Joon

あー、もう何よ
はいはい、私達が
完全憑依異変の黒幕ですよ

私達には絶対勝てない
って噂を聞いてないの？

Geez, what's your problem?
Fine fine, we're the masterminds of
the Perfect Possession Incident.

I'm sure you've heard the rumors that
it's completely impossible to beat us?

Yukari

それはやってみないと
判らないんじゃないかしら？

私は一人でも強いけど
最強の助っ人も連れてきましたよ

そいつが出てきたら
必ず異変は解決するのですから

But you never know
until you try, right?

I'm plenty strong by myself,
but I brought the ultimate supporter.

Once it's her turn,
the incident is as good as resolved.

Shion

ねえ女苑
ザコが何か言ってるよ
切ないねぇ、不幸だねぇ

Hey, Joon. The small fry just keeps
going on and on. It just never ends.
Is this my bad luck?

Joon

哀れすぎて見てられないね
私が吸い取る運すら
持って無さそうね

It's so pitiful I can't bear to watch.
Doesn't seem like she even has
any luck for me to drain.

Music Stops

Joon

ライブのドサクサに紛れて
死んでしまった方が幸せかもね

Maybe you'd be happier slipping into the crowd and then,
I dunno, dropping dead?

Live Starts

BGM:今宵は飄逸なエゴイスト(Live ver)　～Egoistic Flowers.

BGM:Tonight Stars an Easygoing Egoist (Live ver) ~ Egoistic Flowers

Yukari

はっ
お前達の様な生粋の嫌われ者は
本当の幸福を知らない

幸福を知らない者は
性格がひん曲がってしまう

しかし喜ぶがいい！

お前達に完全敗北という
幸福をくれてやろう

歓喜せよ！

人生を変える
スペシャルライブの始まりだ！

Ha...
People born to be hated like you
don't know even know what true happiness is.

You've never known happiness,
so your personalities have become twisted.

But rejoice!

I'll grant you the happiness
they call a total loss.

What joy!

It's time to begin the special concert
that will change your lives!

If player loses

Shion

哀れすぎて見てられない

Okay, now she's too pitiful for me to watch either.

Joon Yorigami PAUSES

Joon

ふふふ
言うだけあって腕が立つようねぇ

でも、そろそろ
遊びは終わりにしようか！

Tee hee hee.
Just from how you talk, I'm guessing you have some skill.

But it's right about time
for playtime to end!

Yukari

アレが来る……
霊夢、準備は良い？

It's coming...
Are you ready, Reimu?

Reimu

よく判らないけど、任せた！

I still don't quite get it, but leave it to me!

Joon

今まさに！
お前達の負けは確定した！

Now's when your fate is sealed!
Your loss is decided!

Joon & Shion

憑依交換「アブソリュートルーザー」

Possession Exchange "Absolute Loser"

Stage 2[edit]

「最強の二人の誤算」

"The Miscalculation of the Strongest Duo"

Shion

よし、取り憑いた！
これで女苑の勝ちは確定した！

……あれ、女苑？
何処に行ったの？

All right, you're possessed!
Now Joon's victory is assured!

...Wait, Joon?
Where'd you go?

BGM:今宵は飄逸なエゴイスト(Live ver)　～Egoistic Flowers.

BGM:Tonight Stars an Easygoing Egoist (Live ver) ~ Egoistic Flowers

Reimu

はー、こういうことね

紫の説明じゃ何が起こるのか
よく判らなかったけど

Oh... so that's what it was.

Yukari's explanation gave me no clue
what was going to happen.

Shion

む、お前
何故自由に動いている？

女苑のスレイブになって
自由を奪われている筈なのに……

Y-You!
How are you moving freely?!

You were supposed to become Joon's slave
and have your freedom taken away...

Reimu

あんたは誰かに取り憑いて
必ず負けさせる事が出来る能力を
持っているのね

それに妹の疫病神は
スレイブを交換する形であんたを
強制的に憑依させる事が出来る

つまり、強制的に対戦相手を
敗北させる事が出来るという訳ね

まさに、最凶最悪の二人ねぇ

でも、真に最強の二人には
敵わなかったね

True, you have the power
to guarantee defeat for
anyone you possess...

And your sister, the plague god,
can switch slaves, forcing you
on someone else...

In other words, you can force defeat on
any opponent. Absolutely.

You really are the worst, most disastrous combo.

But you were no match for
two truly strong people.

Shion

女苑に憑いたお前は身体の自由が
利かなくなるはず……

なのになんで動いている？

You were supposed to lose control of your body
when you possessed Joon...

But how?! How are you still moving?

Reimu

よく見て
あんた、マスター側になってるよ

Look closely.
You're the master now.

Shion

え？

Eh?

Reimu

そして私もマスターよ
紫がマスターとスレイブの境界を
逆にしてくれたの

だから入れ替わったスレイブは
紫とあんたの妹

And I'm a master too.
Yukari flipped the border
of master and slave.

So the partners who were switched
were Yukari and your sister.

Joon

しまった！
嵌められたよ！

私が敵のスレイブに
なっちゃった！

Crap!
It was a trap!

I'm the enemy's slave now!

Shion

なんて事!?

私は紫に取り憑いていないの？
と言うことは
自分のスレイブにはまさか！

What?!

So I'm not possessing Yukari then...?
Wait, then my slave must be-

Yukari

ご名答
これでは疫病神の能力も
貧乏神の能力も意味ないわね

さ、霊夢
私はこれ以上協力は出来ないので
この貧乏神をサクッと倒してね

Right answer!
Like this, both your power as a poverty god
and her power as a plague god are meaningless.

Now Reimu,
I won't be able to offer you any more assistance.
Take care of this poverty god.

Shion

マスターとスレイブを
入れ替えるなんて
そんな事出来る奴がいるとは！

They switched the master and slave?
They can do that?
There's someone who can do that?!

Joon

ああ、もう駄目だー
姉さん一人で勝てるはずがない

戦闘でも全く役立たずだし
根暗で貧乏くさくてしみったれで
自分では何もしようとしないし

文句ばっかり言ってて
何一つ良いところのない
姉さん一人なんてー

大体ねぇ
一度も勝利の経験がない貧乏神が

よりによって異変解決の巫女と
一騎打ちなんてー

もう絶望的だー
完全敗北だー！

Ah, we're done for!
There's no way you can win on your own, sis!

You're totally useless in battle!
You're just a gloomy, poor, smelly, miser
who can't do anything on your own!

All you ever do is complain!
There's not a single good thing about you!
And now you're on your own!

I mean, you're a god of poverty who's
never experienced victory even once.

In a one-on-one fight with the goddamned
incident-resolving shrine maiden!?

It's over!
We lost!

Reimu

辛辣なお別れの挨拶はもういいね

さ、人を負けにする能力以外は
完全に無能のザコ貧乏神を倒して
サクッと異変解決しようっと

Okay okay, that's enough of the harsh farewell.

Now, all I have to do is beat
this small fry poverty god whose
only power is to make people lose.

Shion

女苑、お前達……

みんな馬鹿にしやがって
そんなに言う事ないじゃない！

Joon... All of you...

Even if everyone's always looking down on me,
you didn't have to go that far!

Shion Yorigami Awakens

Shion

私の名は、依神紫苑

泣く子も不幸にする貧乏神だ！

My name is Shion Yorigami!

I'm a poverty god who brings bad luck even to crying children!

Reimu

す、凄い負のオーラ！

Wh-What an incredible negative aura!

Shion

今まで本気を
出さなかったのには訳がある

私が本気を出すと
自分も含めて全ての者が
不幸になるからだ！

もういい
勝ち負けなんて興味は無い

私が本気になった以上
ここにいる全員を負けさせてやる

巫女も私も、女苑、お前もだ！

I've never been serious
about this until now.

If I let it all out,
everyone will be unfortunate,
including me!

But I've had enough.
Who cares about winning or losing?

When I'm done here,
we'll all be losers.

The shrine maiden, me, and you too, Joon!

Reimu

この負のオーラの強さは
疫病神の比じゃないよ！

貧乏神を表舞台に出すこの作戦
失敗だったんじゃない？

This negative aura makes
the plague god seem like nothing!

Did our plan fail when we put
the poverty god in center stage?!

Shion

誰も得をしない
誰も幸せにならない

全員が等しく
不幸の世界を見せてやる！

No one wins.
No one finds happiness.

I'll show you a world where
we all suffer together!

Reimu

これに飲み込まれたら
訳も無く負ける！

気をしっかり持て！
私は異変解決の巫女よ！

If I get swallowed up by this,
I'll lose for no reason!

Pull yourself together!
I'm the incident-resolving shrine maiden!

Shion

貧乏神と疫病神で
最凶最悪の二人だって？

笑わせるな！

最凶最悪は一人で十分
それこそが私
貧乏神の依神紫苑だ！

貧しさに怯えて死ね！

The plague and poverty gods were
the worst, most disastrous combo?

Don't make me laugh!

I was the worst and most disastrous all on my own!
I am the god of poverty,
Shion Yorigami!

Die cowering before poverty!

After breaking Shion's barrier, if on Overdrive difficulty

Shion

うわー！

Uwaaa!

Reimu

何これ！
急に空気が変わったわ！

What is this!
Suddenly the atmosphere turns different!

Shion

……私の溜め込んだオーラが
みんな出てしまう！

...The auras I've been retaining,
they're all flowing out!

Reimu

負のオーラに紛れて
こんな眩しいオーラを
隠し持っていたのね！

So you've been covering that glaring aura
under your negative ones all along!

Shion

こうなりゃ破れかぶれだ！
持てる限りのオーラを
出し切ってやる！

I don't care anymore!
I'm going all-in with every aura I have!

Reimu

貴方のお陰で
無限に力が湧いてくるわ！

もう負ける気がしない！
やっぱり貴方は
自らの能力で負けるのよ！

By your grace,
my power is flowing like the endless tide!

Now the victory is in my hands!
Just like what I thought, you lost to your own ability!

Shion Yorigami DEFEATED

Yukari and Reimu Ending
