None other! A high-class human

Ichirin Kumoi and Nitori Kawashiro DEFEATED

You're really getting
the hang of it!
We're definitely the ultimate combo!

Tenshi

Hee hee hee.
Speaking of inchlings, your kind can trace their lineage
back to a wise and skilled race of nobility.

I'm so glad I met an excellent
partner worthy of me.

「天人と夢の住人」

"A Celestial and a Dream Dweller"

こいつは？

Who's this?

Shinmyoumaru

お面の妖怪
私もよく判らないけど
付喪神の一種よ

A mask youkai.
I don't know much either,
but she's a kind of tsukumogami.

Tenshi

付喪神か
これまた下賤な奴だな

A tsukumogami, huh?
Another crass peasant then.

秦こころ

Hata no Kokoro

Kokoro

誰？

Who?

ドレミー・スイート

Doremy Sweet

Doremy

彼女は、天人です
最近下らない理由で天界を

She's a celestial. She hasn't been down here
recently because she was under house arrest in Heaven,
but now she's been banished.

Tenshi

な？
何故そんなことを知っている？

Wha-
How do you know that?!

Doremy

夢の世界の貴方が
愚痴ってましたよ

Because you're always whining about it
in the Dream World.

Tenshi

夢の世界だと？
お前は一体……

Dream World?!
Just what are you?

Doremy

私はドレミースイート
夢の支配者です

I am Doremy Sweet,
the ruler of dreams.

Hata no Kokoro.

とても天上の人とは思えません

この様な感情が醜い者の存在は

This person has only ugly feelings.
I can't picture her being a resident of Heaven.

I can't accept the existence of such
an ugly person with feelings like that!

BGM:Shinkirou Theatrical

Kokoro

Just die already!

If player loses

Kokoro

甘やかされて育ったんでしょうね
感情のコントロールが

I'm sure you were spoiled as a child.
You have no control over your emotions.

Hata no Kokoro and Doremy Sweet DEFEATED

Tenshi

夢の世界の連中だって？
弱い奴ほどよく吠える

You said you were from the Dream World?
You sure bark a lot for toothless mutts.

Shinmyoumaru

こいつ初めて見たー
夢の支配者って、本当？

I've never seen this one before!
Are you really the ruler of dreams?!

Doremy

ええ、勿論です

Yes, of course.

Shinmyoumaru

じゃあ、さっき言ってた
天界を追放されたってのは本当？

Then, about what you said before...
Is it true that she was banished from Heaven?

Doremy

本人がいるのですから
聞いてみたら如何です？

She's right here.
Why not ask her yourself?

Tenshi

まあ、追放は本当だよ
といっても理不尽な理由でさー

私は生粋の天人で、生まれながら
偉いからなんも仕事しなくても
良いんだけどさー

暇だから美味しくもない催事用の
丹 (たん) をつまみ食いしただけなのに
みんな大騒ぎで

催事の準備の邪魔だって
暫く地上に放り出されたの
理不尽じゃない？

Well, it's true I was banished.
But it was for such a ridiculous reason.

As a natural-born celestial,
destined for greatness from birth,
why should I have to work?

And all I did was eat some disgusting
elixir they were using in
the great hall when I was on break.

Said I was "getting in the way."
So they kicked me out until they finish
preparing for the event. Isn't that ridiculous?

Shinmyoumaru

あ、うん
り、理不尽、なのかな？

Ah... yeah.
I-Is that really ridiculous?

Kokoro

理不尽だと思う理由が判らない

I don't understand why you think that's ridiculous.

Notes[edit]

  • The Shinmyoumaru from Dream World has a resemblance to the real Shinmyoumaru's previous partner Seija Kijin, on the vulgar style of speech. Seija indeed made the Reversal Incident to replace the upper class with the lower, which is similar to Tenshi's destructive desire in this work.


This page is part of Project Translations, a Touhou Wiki project that aims to ensure the quality of translations of all official material in the Touhou Project.
