Kokoro defeats Reimu[edit]

Kokoro

ねえねえ、神社で能やりたいわー
また呼んでよー

Hey hey, I want to do a
Noh performance at the shrine!
Invite me over again!

Kokoro defeats Marisa[edit]

Kokoro

改めてみると
魔法の星空綺麗だわー！

I never noticed before,
but your magical starry sky is so pretty!

Kokoro defeats Ichirin[edit]

Kokoro

入道さん
大きいなぁ、見越せないなぁ

Mister Nyuudou sure is big, huh?
He's unpredictable, too.

Kokoro defeats Byakuren[edit]

Kokoro

凄いっすね
呪文で強くなれるなんて
憧れますわー

Wow, cool!
Getting stronger by chanting?
I want to be like you!

Kokoro defeats Futo[edit]

Kokoro

帽子が重いのですかー？

Isn't that hat heeeeavy?

Kokoro defeats Miko[edit]

Kokoro

最近、感情が溢れ出る様になって
ちょっとした事で感動するのー
毎日が楽しいですわー！

I've been sooo overflowing with emotions lately, I'm getting fired up by even by the smallest of things!
Every day's been fun!

Kokoro defeats Nitori[edit]

Kokoro

戦闘はまだまだですが
商売は上手ですねー！
見習いたいです

You could work on your fighting skills,
but you're really good as business!
I should learn from you!

Kokoro defeats Koishi[edit]

Kokoro

負けても楽しそうな笑顔
素敵ですー！

You look so happy even though you lost!
It's a wonderful smile!

Kokoro defeats Mamizou[edit]

Kokoro

酒に煙草をやりながら戦闘ですか
大人ですねー！

Drinking and smoking in the middle of a fight?
You're so mature!

Kokoro defeats herself[edit]

Kokoro

ん？　私がもう一人？
こういう時はどういう
表情をすれば良かったんだっけ？

Hm? Another me?
What expression was I
supposed to show here again?

Kokoro defeats Kasen[edit]

Kokoro

動物って可愛いです！
無口で、表情もよく判らなくて

The animals are so cute!
They're all quiet,
and I can't read their expressions.

Kokoro defeats Mokou[edit]

Kokoro

死なないのって凄い事ですよね！

Not being able to die is amazing!

Kokoro defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Kokoro

私もお椀の中に入ってみたい！
私がスレイブになりたいわー

I want to get in the bowl too!
I want to be your slave!

Kokoro defeats Sumireko[edit]

Kokoro

そのマントの裏地、派手ですねー！

The lining of your cloak is so flashy!

Kokoro defeats Reisen[edit]

Kokoro

手に持っているそれ
大きなラッパですか？
いい音がしそうですねー

Is that a big trumpet you're holding?
It looks like it can make some nice sounds!

Kokoro defeats Doremy[edit]

Kokoro

夢の世界って
なんで感情が何倍にも
膨れ上がるんでしょうねぇ

So why do emotions get many times
stronger in the Dream World anyway?

Kokoro

あれ？
貴方は何処かで
会っていたはず……

ああ、そうだった
私は夢の世界の
秦こころだったのを忘れてた

Huh?
I could swear I've met you somewhere before...?

Oh, of course! I forgot that
I'm the Hata no Kokoro of the Dream World!

Kokoro defeats Tenshi[edit]

Kokoro

そこまで自信満々だと
こっちも気持ちよくなりますー

Seeing you with so much self-condifidence
makes me me feel better too!

Kokoro defeats Yukari[edit]

Kokoro

その空間の切れ目の向こう側
恐ろしいけど見てみたい……

That space on the
other side of your gap is scary,
but I want to take a peek...

Kokoro defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Kokoro

うわー、お金持ちそう！
私にも奢って奢ってー

Wow, you look so rich!
Buy me something, buy me something!

Against Shion

Kokoro

うわー、貧しそう！
お金持ちっぽい妹さんから
お金を貸して貰ったら？

Wow, you look so poor!
Can't you borrow money
from your rich sister?

Generic Victory[edit]

Kokoro

うわーい
戦えるの楽しーわー！

Yaay!
Fighting is fuuuun!

Kokoro works together with Doremy for a slave[edit]

Kokoro

私は夢の世界の秦こころ
現 (うつつ) の世界で暴れるの
楽しみにしてたんだ

I'm Hata no Kokoro of the Dream World.
I've been wanting to rampage
in the real world!
