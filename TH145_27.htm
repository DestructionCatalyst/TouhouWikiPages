Mokou defeats Reimu[edit]

Mokou

お前か？
私にオカルトを押しつけたのは

Was it you?
The one who dragged me into this Occult nonsense.

Mokou

そう言えば
永遠亭の連中に
ちょっとだけ聞いたぜ

お前
厄介で悪夢でしかない仕事を
押し付けられそうだぞ？

Now that you mention it,
I did some asking around
over at Eientei.

Seems like you're
about to get dragged into some troublesome,
nightmarish job.[1]

Mokou defeats Marisa[edit]

Mokou

止めときな
不老不死は孤独で
救われないもんだ

Give it up.
Immortality is lonely
and inescapable.

Mokou defeats Ichirin[edit]

Mokou

僧侶が人間を救っていたなら
この世はとっくに極楽だった

If monks had actually been saving people,
this world would’ve become a paradise long ago.

Mokou defeats Byakuren[edit]

Mokou

どうだ？
死にまくった私の為に
御経でも読んでくれないかね

Well?
Won’t you read a sutra for me,
the one dying over and over?

Mokou defeats Futo[edit]

Mokou

お菊さんか……
成仏できなくても
死んでいる事には変わりない

Okiku-san, huh?
Even if she couldn't reach enlightenment,
that doesn't change the fact that she's dead.

Mokou defeats Miko[edit]

Mokou

お前も不老不死だと……？

You're immortal too, you say...?

Mokou

自ら不老不死になる奴の
気が知れん

I have no idea what the hell you people attaining immortality are thinking.

Mokou defeats Nitori[edit]

Mokou

全ての水分を蒸発させてやる

I'll evaporate every last drop of moisture from you.

Mokou defeats Koishi[edit]

Mokou

地底の妖怪か
どうりで奇怪な奴だと思った

You’re a youkai from underground?
No wonder I thought you were an unusual one.

Mokou defeats Mamizou[edit]

Mokou

その尻尾はよく燃えそうだな

That tail looks like it'd burn up nicely.

Mokou defeats Kokoro[edit]

Mokou

顔が笑ってないな？
お前怖いぞ

Your face isn't smiling, you know?
You're scary.

Mokou defeats herself[edit]

Mokou

今更何が起きても驚か……
うわあ、自分だあ！

After all this time, there’s nothing left that can scare m-
Wah, that’s me!

Mokou defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Mokou

お前だけ大きな防具を
身につけているな
正々堂々と勝負しろ！

You’re the only one out of us wearing a big shield.
Take that off and fight fairly!

Mokou defeats Kasen[edit]

Mokou

そうか、仙人は死神と闘って
長寿を得ているんだってな

I see, so what you’re saying is that
hermits gain longevity by fighting off shinigami.

Mokou defeats Sumireko[edit]

Mokou

夢の中？
この間は実際にこっちに
いたじゃ無いか

You’re dreaming?
But weren’t you visiting for real just the other day?

Mokou

この間の忘れ物は届いたか？

そうか
あれはチェインメールだったのか
ってなんだそれ？

Did you get sent that lost thing from the other day?

I see.
Oh, so it was a chain letter.
...wait, what?

Mokou defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Mokou

また月の連中か
勘弁してくれよ
捨て身で襲撃してやろうか？

The Lunarian bunch again?
Give me a break.
Should I take one of my lives and attack that place?

Generic victory[edit]

Mokou

オカルトなんて
私には関係無いのに

I don't even care
about this Occult nonsense...

Mokou

この程度のイベント
腐るほど味わって来たよ

I’ve had way too damn much of these kinda happenings.

Mokou

どうやら……

私に人体発火現象という
都市伝説を押しつけた奴が
いるという話っぽいな

It would appear that....

There's someone who's stuck me with the Urban Legend of spontaneous combustion.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Mokou is likely foreshadowing the events of Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.
