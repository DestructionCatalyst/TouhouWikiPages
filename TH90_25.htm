Tewi defeats Reimu[edit]

Tewi

そうそう、霊夢は鳥居の向きを東じゃなくて
南向きにすれば、もっと幸運が訪れるわー。

That's right, if Reimu makes the torii face south instead of east,
more good luck will come to her~

Tewi

もっとお賽銭にお金が入る方法を
知りたくない？

知りたくない。
そう。

Don't you want to know how to earn more donations?

You don't.
Okay.

Tewi defeats Marisa[edit]

Tewi

魔理沙は、森の外に家を構えれば
幸せになるわー。

If Marisa built her home outside the forest, she would receive good luck~

Tewi defeats Sakuya[edit]

Tewi

咲夜は、今の住処を捨てて独立すれば、
幸せになるわー。

If Sakuya left the mansion behind and lived alone, she would receive good luck~

Tewi defeats Youmu[edit]

Tewi

妖夢は、その剣で自分を突いてみると、
もっと幸せになるわー。

If Youmu stabbed herself with that sword, she would receive greater good luck~

Tewi defeats Reisen[edit]

Tewi

ああ！
まだちょっとやることがあるから待ってー！

Ah!
I've still got a few more things to do, so just wait!

Tewi defeats Cirno[edit]

Tewi

もっと高級な蛙を手に入れてみない？
いい方法があるんだけど……

Do you want to catch frogs of higher rank?
I might have some good ideas....
[Perhaps unrelated, but Cirno would later get swallowed by a giant toad.]

Tewi defeats Lyrica[edit]

Tewi

ああ、貴方は随分と恵まれた生活をしている
のね。幸せがほくほくと伝わってくるわ。

Ah, you're living a pretty blessed lifestyle, aren't you. I'm feeling your happiness warmly over here.

Tewi defeats Merlin[edit]

Tewi

ソロでライブをすることもあるんだって？
じゃあ、宣伝は私に任せてよ。

You're also planning a solo live concert?
Then leave the promotions to me.

Tewi defeats Lunasa[edit]

Tewi

随分と古いヴァイオリンね。
ストラディヴァリって知ってる？

That's quite an old violin.
Do you know Stradivari?

Tewi defeats Mystia[edit]

Tewi

そうねぇ。私に考えがあるから、新しい商売を
始めると良いと思うわ。

You see, I've thought this through.
It would be best if you started a new business, I think.
[Mystia would later open a grilled lamprey stand.]

Tewi defeats herself[edit]

Tewi

この辺には可愛い兎が多いね。

There sure are a lot of cute rabbits around here.

Tewi defeats Aya[edit]

Tewi

貴方の新聞の購読者を増やしたくない？
私に良い考えがあるんだけれど……

Do you want to increase your paper's subscriptions?
I've got a good idea but....
[Aya would later give out free extras to increase her subscriptions in Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red.]

Tewi defeats Medicine[edit]

Tewi

毒は一種の財宝ね。
だから、ちゃんと管理しないと……

どう？
私に管理を任せてみない？

Poison is a kind of treasure,
so if you don't take care of it well....

How about it?
Why don't you let me keep them safe for you?

Tewi defeats Yuuka[edit]

Tewi

この異変を自分の物にしてみない？
貴方ならそれが出来ると思うわ。

Why don't you make this incident as if it's your own?
I think you should be able to do that.

Tewi defeats Komachi[edit]

Tewi

そんな投げる位お金を持ってどうするの？

What are you going to do with your money, having so much that you can throw them away like this?

Tewi defeats Eiki[edit]

Tewi

ほら、私はみんなを幸せにしています。
私は、地獄へ行かなくて済むでしょう？

See, I'm making everyone attain happiness.
Do I redeem myself from hell now?

Tewi defeats someone[edit]

Tewi

みんな、私の言うこと聞けばもっと幸せに
なれますよー。

Everyone, if you listen to what I have to say you'll be more happy~

Tewi

もっと幸せになりたければ、大きな気持ちを
もつ事ね。

If you want to be happier, it means holding deeper emotions.
