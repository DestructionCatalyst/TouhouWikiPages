Sakuya beats Reimu[edit]

Sakuya

挨拶もその辺にして、お茶の時間にしましょうか。

Well, let's stop with the greetings and have tea, shall we?

Sakuya beats Marisa[edit]

Sakuya

そんな力の使い方じゃあ、カロリーの無駄使いよ。
それとも、ダイエットかしら？

You're wasting calories by using so much strength.
Or, are you on a diet?

Sakuya beats Alice[edit]

Sakuya

そんなに一度に人形を操って……
でも、手先の器用さなら私も負けないわよ。

To be able to control so many dolls at once...
I won't lose at dexterity, though.

Sakuya beats Patchouli[edit]

Sakuya

あら、今日の紅茶……
お気に召さなかったのでしょうか？

Oh, today's red tea...
Did it not please you?

Sakuya beats Youmu[edit]

Sakuya

長剣と投げナイフ、どちらがリーチがあるか……
すぐに判るような気がするけど。

A longsword and throwing knives... Which one has better reach?
I think I know already.[1]

Sakuya beats Remilia[edit]

Sakuya

さぁ、お戯れはその辺にして……

Okay, stop with the games...

Sakuya

そう言えば、気付いてます？

この妖気……
そろそろ原因を突詰めないといけないわ。

Speaking of which... did you realize?

We have to find out the reason
for this mist soon or else.

Sakuya beats Suika[edit]

Sakuya

罰として、私の代わりにナイフを回収しなさい。
貴方なら簡単でしょう？

As punishment, go retrieve my knives.
It's easy for you, right?

Sakuya beats Meiling[edit]

Sakuya

仕事は余りサボらないことね
私と違って貴方の時間は有限だもの

Don't slack off.
Unlike me, you have limited time.

Sakuya beats someone[edit]

Sakuya

立つ鳥跡を濁さず。
急いでナイフの回収をしないと……

It's customary to clean up after oneself.
I have to retrieve my knives quickly...

Sakuya

貴方の時間は私のもの。
時間以外のものを貴方が持っていればいいけど。

Your time is mine.
Well, it'd be nice if you had something else besides time, but...

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ The comparison of sword and knife appears in Youmu's script.
