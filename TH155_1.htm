Stage 1[edit]

「純粋に楽しむ二人」

"The Pure Fun Duo"

BGM:　憑依投合

BGM:Being Things Eye To Eye

Reimu

さてと
何処から調査しようかしら

Let's see.
What should we start looking at first?

Kasen

誰がこんな噂を広めたのかを
探るには内側に飛び込むしか
ないわね

具体的には
自ら完全憑依を行って戦う

To know our enemy who spread this rumor,
first, we must know the inside out of
this incident they caused.

To be specific,
we're going to use Perfect Possession
on ourselves to fight.

Reimu

手掛かりが無いしそれしか無いね

となるとやっぱり最初は……

There's no better things to do when we have no clues anyway.

But I'm sure we can start with...

霧雨魔理沙
高火力で星好きな魔法使い

Marisa Kirisame
A High Firepower and Star-Loving Magician

Marisa

よう
暇だから一戦交えようぜー！

Hey!
Wanna do a quick match to kill some time?

Reimu

良いところに来たわね

完全憑依調査の
最初の被検体として相応しいわ

You're right on time.

The perfect first subject for
the Perfect Possession investigation!

Marisa

被検体？
何の話だ？

Subject?
What's that about?

Kasen

悪いけど
今日は二対一で行かせて貰うわ

Sorry,
but today it's gonna be a two versus one.

Marisa

わあ、入れ替わった！
完全憑依だな？

Whoa, did you two just swap?
That's Perfect Possession, right?

Reimu

実際に利用してみて
真相を探る事にしたの！

Yep. We're finding the truth behind it
by actually using it!

Kasen

ちょっと、待って
魔理沙、既に……

Wait a second, Marisa,
you've already...

古明地こいし
意図せずに心を閉ざしたサトリ

Koishi Komeiji
An Aimless and Guarded Satori

Koishi

あら、ここは何処かしら？

Um, hello? Where am I?

Reimu

あんたも既に
完全憑依されてたー!?

You've been under Perfect Possession all along!

BGM: 地の色は黄色　～ Primrose

BGM: The Ground's Color is Yellow ~ Primrose

If player loses

Marisa

何で私が完全憑依されてたんだ？

How did I get perfect possessed?

Marisa Kirisame DEFEATED

Marisa

おい、これは一体何なんだ？
自分が別人と入れ替わったような

Hey, what exactly is this?
I feel like I just swapped to another person.

Reimu

あんたは気が付いて
いなかったのね

肉体も精神も入れ替わる
これが完全憑依と呼ばれる物よ

You didn't notice anything
that happened to you, huh.

The interchange of both body and mind.
That's what we called "Perfect Possession."

Marisa

完全憑依の噂は聞いていたが
こんな簡単に入れ替われるのか

これは面白……
いや不思議な

I heard about the rumor,
but I never thought it would be that easy.

That's pretty interes...
uh, more like, mysterious.

Stage 2[edit]

「強さを求める二人」

"The Stength-Seeking Duo"

BGM: 連帯責人

BGM: The One Jointly Responsible

Kasen

さっきの魔理沙で
気付いたことがある

I noticed something
from Marisa earlier.

Reimu

何？

What's that?

Kasen

私達がやっているような
合意の元で完全憑依を
実行する以外に

マスターが無自覚のまま
完全憑依出来る場合もあると
言うこと

Perfect Possession could take
place by mutual consent, just
like what we've been doing.

But now we know that in some cases,
Perfect Possession could occur while
bypassing the master's consciousness.

Reimu

そうね
私が初めて完全憑依されたときも
無自覚だったわ

Yeah,
I was totally unaware of it when I was perfect possessed.[1]

Kasen

と言うことは悪用すると
一方的に身体を乗っ取れる
という事にも繋がるのでは……

If that's the case,
it could be abused as a tool
to one-sidedly take over anyone's body...

Reimu

それは……厄介な予想ねぇ

That's... a horrific hypothesis.

二ツ岩マミゾウ
変幻自在で喰えない化け狸

Mamizou Futatsuiwa
A Surreal and Cunning Bake-Danuki

Mamizou

おや
お前さんが出てきたと言う事は

早速調査を開始したって事かのう

Ha.
The fact you people are here...

Means that you had started your own investigation. As quick as usual.

藤原妹紅
自暴自棄で不死身の人間

Fujiwara no Mokou
A Desperate and Immortal Human

Mokou

もっとこの異変を長く楽しんでも
良いんじゃないか？

上手くすれば
無尽蔵に暴れられるぜ

Why would you want this incident to end so fast?
Don't you see how fun this is?

You can go as crazy as you want
once you get good at it.

Reimu

さすがの嗅覚ね

もう完全憑依を利用している
奴らが居るとはね

As perceptive as always.

And we've already had ones like you taking advantage of Perfect Possession.
How terrific.

Mamizou

ま、今更儂らを調べている
ところを見るに

この異変について
全く判っていないようじゃがな

I see.
You're still picking on us without realizing how late you are.

Which means you literally know nothing about this incident.

Mokou

この異変
もっと楽しませて貰うぞ！

Let's enjoy ourselves in this incident
a little bit more!

BGM: 不滅のレッドソウル

BGM:Immortal Red Soul

If player loses

Mamizou

この完全憑依を
上手く使うには
コツがいるようじゃな

Looks like you don't know the trick of Perfect Possession yet.

Mamizou Futatsuiwa DEFEATED

Reimu

完全憑依異変を
既にもう悪用している奴が
いたなんて

We've already had abusers
at this stage of the incident.
Unbelievable.

Mamizou

何を言っている
もうみんなに広まっているぞい

ある手順を踏めば
簡単に憑依できるとな

What are you talking about?
Everyone should know the trick by now.

The trick that allows you
to easily possess anyone.

Kasen

ある手順……
それは瞑想ね

The trick...
You mean meditation?

Mamizou

それもそうじゃが……

マスター側が合意しているか
油断しているかすれば

瞑想状態に入るだけで
憑依できるんじゃ

簡単すぎるじゃろ？

Well that's just one way to do it.

Either the master consents to it,
or you look for moments when they're distracted.

Then you can just go ahead and possess them
by entering meditative state.

It's almost too easy, isn't it?

Stage 3[edit]

「最強超人の二人」

"The Strongest Superhuman Duo"

BGM: 合縁奇縁

BGM: An Odd Couple

豊聡耳神子
全能で人の為に動く道士

Toyosatomimi no Miko
An Almighty and Serving Hermit

Miko

なる程、お前も動き始めたか

I see. You are taking action as well.

Reimu

今のところ
何の見当も付かないけどね

まあ、そのうち
何とかなるんじゃない？

There's still nothing in progress though.

We're gonna figure things out when the time comes.
Probably.

聖白蓮
超人的で悟りを開く大阿闍梨

Byakuren Hijiri
A Superhuman and Enlightened Great Acharya

Hijiri

頼りにならない巫女ねぇ

What an unreliable shrine maiden.

Miko

我々もこの異変の調査に
すでに動いておる

その為に今回コンビを組んだのだ
我々の邪魔をしないでくれるか

We've started our own investigation aside from yours.

And that's why we're teamed up.
Now, could you at least not get in our way?

Reimu

そんなわけに行くもんか

行き当たりばったりでも
異変は私が解決するよ！

No way that's gonna happen.

Resolving incidents is my job and I'll finish it no matter how reckless it's gonna be!

BGM: 光輝く天球儀

BGM: Shining Armillary Sphere

If player loses

Miko

さて、いよいよ
我ら最強異変調査隊の活動開始だ

Finally, it's time for the strongest
incident investigation team to move!

Toyosatomimi no Miko DEFEATED

Miko

なる程
完全憑依は使いこなすのに
コツがいる様だな

I see.
I'm a few techniques away from mastering Perfect Possession.

Reimu

あんたら二人じゃ、我が強すぎて
協調できてないんじゃない？

You two are too egoistic to cooperate.
That's where the problem is.

Miko

それもあるな
私一人でも良いのだが

この異変は完全憑依しないと
調査は難しいと感じ
仕方が無く組んでいるだけだ

協調なんて無用である

You may be right.
I could've done it better all by myself.

I felt the use of Perfect Possession
would be necessary for this investigation.
So I had no choice but to form a team.

Turns out that cooperation is simply a waste of efforts.

Kasen

いつも和を尊べ
って言ってなかったっけ？

Weren't you always saying
that harmony is to be valued?

Miko

それは
我に逆らうなと言う意味だ

That was just a warning
to those who would oppose me.

Kasen

ああ、そういう……

Oh, now I get it...

Stage 4[edit]

「一匹狼は敵か味方か」

"Is the Lone Wolf, a Foe or Friend?"

BGM: 知略縦横

BGM: Scheming Outside the Box

Kasen

みんな完全憑依の噂に
振り回されているようね

It seems like everyone is getting fooled around by
this rumor of Perfect Possession.

Reimu

今日の調査では
大したことは判らなかったわ

調査は長期に及びそうね

We didn't manage to learn anything important
after today's investigation, either.

It will be a long battle, I suppose.

Kasen

いや……
まだ今日は終わらないみたいよ

...And today is gonna be a long day.

Yukari Yakumo Enters

Yukari

完全憑依を利用しての調査……

それは悪意で仕組まれた
最悪の選択の一つよ

Investigation that makes use of Perfect Possession...

Which is the worst, carefully crafted trap with pure malice.
And you failed to choose the right way.

BGM: 憑坐は夢と現の間に　～ Necro-Fantasia

BGM: Yorimashi Between Dreams and Reality ~ Necro-Fantasia

Reimu

紫 (あんた) ……！

このタイミングで出てくる
と言う事は
また、あんたが絡んでいるのね！

Yukari (You) ...!
The fact that you come out at such timing,
means that you must be related to the incident,
again!

Yukari

強制的に身体を乗っ取れるように
するなんて、悪魔の所業……

いや、邪道の極みだわ
この私がそんな事をするように
見えるのかしら

Forcibly possessing one's body is
a deed of devils...

Or I should say, the pinnacle of all evils.
Do I really look like someone
who would commit such an act?

Reimu

見えるよ！
見えすぎる！

異変に関係しているとしか
考えられないわ！

You do look like it!
You look way too much like you would!

It would make no sense if you were not involved!

Before final card, if on Overdrive difficulty

Reimu

まるで手応えがない……

That was suspiciously easy...

Yukari

愚かな……完全憑依の危険が
貴方達には判らないのね

ならば、憑坐 (よりまし) は悪意 (だんまく) で学べ！
弾幕馬鹿達よ！

How foolish...
You don't understand the dangers
behind Perfect Possession.

Then the Yorimashi (mediums) will have to learn via malice (danmaku) !
You danmaku-addled fools!

If player loses

Yukari

今のままでは
黒幕に勝つ手段は見えてこない
状況は最悪ね

We won't be able to deal with the mastermind
if that's all what you've got.
This can't be any worse.

Yukari Yakumo DEFEATED

Main Story Prelude[edit]

幻想郷各所で『完全憑依』と呼ばれる

不可思議な現象が起こっていた。

全くの別人が、精神のみならず
肉体をも乗っ取る現象である。

この物語は、『完全憑依』を監視する者、
利用する者、訳もわからず巻き込まれる者、

そして裏に潜んだ脅威を曝こうとする者……。

これから始まるのは「最強の二人は自分達だ」

と誤解した者達の悲喜交々のストーリーである。		 A mysterious phenomenon known as "Perfect Possession"

is occurring all over Gensokyo.

A phenomenon where one is taken over by a completely different person,
 not only in mind, but also in body.

This is a story of those who observe it,
 those who make use of it, those who are simply caught up in it...

and those preparing to expose the menace lurking behind the scenes.

The bittersweet story of those who mistook themselves

for the strongest duo is about to begin.
