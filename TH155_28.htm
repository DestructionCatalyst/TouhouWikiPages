Tenshi defeats Reimu[edit]

Tenshi

また神社を壊してやろうか？

Should I destroy the shrine again?
For old time's sake?

Tenshi defeats Marisa[edit]

Tenshi

魔法なんて邪悪も邪悪！
失せろ道を外れた者よ

Magic is the most wicked of the wicked!
Begone, heretic!

Tenshi defeats Ichirin[edit]

Tenshi

雲虫が
雲上の天人に敵うと思うなよ

Don't expect a cloud bug to be a match
for a celestial, from above the clouds.

Tenshi defeats Byakuren[edit]

Tenshi

悟ったって、天界に来られる訳
じゃないのにね

They won't let you into heaven just
'cause you're enlightened, you know.

Tenshi defeats Futo[edit]

Tenshi

お前は仙人なのか
なら精進せい！
位無きを患 (うれ) えず、所以を患えよ！

So you're a hermit? Then devote yourself to it!
Don’t be worried about how low you are.
Consider how to get higher!

Tenshi defeats Miko[edit]

Tenshi

仙人か、お前の考え方は古い！
智はなお水の如し
流れざる時は則ち腐るぞ！

Hermit, your narrowed mind ceased the flow of the wisdom!
Knowledge is like water, it rots when it stagnates!

Tenshi defeats Nitori[edit]

Tenshi

崖の下に棲む者よ
哀れよのう

Those who live under a cliff.
I feel pity for you!

Tenshi defeats Koishi[edit]

Tenshi

地底の妖怪？
地面より下に棲む奴なんて
地獄に堕ちた奴位じゃないのか？

Youkai from Underworld?
Those who live one layer below the earth?
You sure you aren't as same as those who were damned to hell?

Tenshi defeats Mamizou[edit]

Tenshi

ぷぷー狸が化ける、だって
古典的すぎてウケるー

Oh, no! The tanukis! They're FAKE!
Aww. The fact it's so classic makes it even funnier.

Tenshi defeats Kokoro[edit]

Tenshi

感情を奪うのか……
私の緋想の術に似てるなぁ

You can take away emotions...
almost like my Sword of Hisou.

Tenshi defeats Kasen[edit]

Tenshi

仙人か、お前は……
道は近きにあり、而るに遠きに求む
何か回りくどいことをしていない？

The path of duty lies in what is near,
and men seek for it in what is remote.
Hermit, haven't you made the same mistake?

Tenshi defeats Mokou[edit]

Tenshi

どうだ？
不老不死を堪能しているー？

So how do you feel?
Has immortality offered you anything good so far?

Tenshi defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Tenshi

小人とは驚いたね
高貴な方が、地上で遊んでるとは

An Inchling!
I'm so surprised that a graceful
being like you is playing on Earth.

Tenshi defeats Sumireko[edit]

Tenshi

天界より高いところに住んでいる
人間がいるんだって？
ほほう、打ち落としてやろうか

Humans who live beyond Heaven?
Interesting.
Interesting enough for me to knock it down.

Tenshi defeats Reisen[edit]

Tenshi

月の都と縁を切ったんだって？
そんな勿体ない話あるもんか

You broke up with the Lunar Capital?
What a shame.

Tenshi defeats Doremy[edit]

Tenshi

夢の世界なんて
そんなもん表に出てくる
筈がない

Dream World?
What does it have to do with the real world?

Tenshi defeats herself[edit]

Tenshi

誰だ？
私の格好を真似している奴は

Who are you?
Why are you mimicking me?

Tenshi defeats Yukari[edit]

Tenshi

ふん、地上はボロボロだねぇ
いい気味だ！

Hey, look at your land, it's all desecrated!
How wonderful it feels!

Tenshi defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Tenshi

そんな見せかけのキラキラ
羨ましくなんて無いね

I have no jealousy in your pursued prettiness.

Against Shion

Tenshi

貧相だねぇ
これぞ私が望む地上の民の姿だ

Oh, what a poor pleb.
I wish every earthling looked like you.

Generic Victory[edit]

Tenshi

近づくとあぶないぜ！
今の私は虫の居所が悪いんだ！

You'd better stay away from me!
I'm in a bad mood!

Tenshi

二人心を同じくすれば
その利、金を断つ
お前達はどうだい？

"When two people join their hearts
they can even cut metal."
So, what about you two?

Tenshi

ひれ伏すが良い！
地面を這い蹲ってる
虫けらどもめ！

Lie on the ground all you want!
Grovel in the dirt, insect!
