Tenshi wins Reimu[edit]

Tenshi 貴方は真っ直ぐすぎるね。曲全、つまり曲がっている
からこそ人生を全う出来ると心得よ 		You are too straight forward. Compromise makes completeness. That is, if you bend your ways you can make your life more complete.
[From Daodejing]

Tenshi wins Marisa[edit]

Tenshi 何やら欲の奴隷となって物を集めているようね
人生を愉しみたければ貪らざるをもって宝と為せ 		Seems like you've become a slave of desire by collecting things.
If you want to find joy in life you should cherish the rejection of greed.
[Second line from Zuo Commentary]

Tenshi wins Sakuya[edit]

Tenshi 貴方がいくら努力しようと、悪魔の下では負の方向に
しか働かないよ 		No matter how hard you work, all that effort is going the wrong way if you're working under the devil.

Tenshi wins Alice[edit]

Tenshi 人形を群と為すのならば、その戦略戦術は水の姿に
学べ。臨機応変が勝利の鍵なのよ 		If you want your dolls to act in a group, then study the form of water for strategies and tactics.
Adaptiveness is the key to victory.
[Repeating what Sun Tzu advocates in the Art of War]

Tenshi wins Patchouli[edit]

Tenshi お前の読んでいる物は古人の糟魄(そうはく)のみ
本当に大切な事は本には記されていない 		What you're reading is only the scraps of the ancients.
The truly important things don't get recorded.
[Quoting the text of Zhuangzi]

Tenshi wins Youmu[edit]

Tenshi 好死は悪活にしかず。どんなに悪い生き方でも、死ぬ
よりは良い。幽霊とはいえ生を軽んじるな 		A clean death is no better than a grim life. No matter how dismal your life is, it is better than death. Don't make light of life, phantom.
[From the Tongzupian, a Qing Dynasty collection of Chinese proverbs.]

Tenshi wins Remilia[edit]

Tenshi いつまでも部下が付いてくると思ってはいけないよ It's not good to think that your subordinates will follow you forever.

Tenshi wins Yuyuko[edit]

Tenshi 飽食終日、何も頭を使う事もない様では亡霊とはいえ
救いようがないね 		There's no salvation for a ghost who fills her stomach all day and doesn't use her head for anything!

[Partly referring to the Analects.]

Tenshi wins Yukari[edit]

Tenshi 清流の清濁はその源にある
幻想郷の妖怪達がチャランポランなのは貴方の所為ね 		The murkiness of the creek has its source.
You are the reason why the youkai of Gensokyo are so irresponsible.
[Quoting the Zhenguan Zhengyao.]

Tenshi wins Suika[edit]

Tenshi 酒は微酔に呑め
酔っぱらってフラフラになるような呑み方は最悪よ 		Drink while half drunk.
Getting wasted by binge drinking is the worst.
[First line from Caigentan]

Tenshi wins Reisen[edit]

Tenshi 懐と安とは実に名を敗る
貴方は安心を求め成長を妨げているように見える 		Leisure and comfort are the ruin of reputation.
By seeking peace of heart, I see you are hindering your own growth.
[First line from Zuo Commentary]

Tenshi wins Aya[edit]

Tenshi ゴシップは智にあらず
興味だけでは新聞は社会にとって負の働きを為すわ 		Gossip is not wisdom.
If it is only written out of curiosity, newspapers make a negative effect on society.

Tenshi wins Komachi[edit]

Tenshi 良いね！
何度私の下に来ようと、負ける事はない！ 		Good!
No matter how many times you come, I won't lose!
Tenshi お前はまだ死神の中では口が下手なようだねぇ

他の死神はもっともっと、揺さぶりを掛けてくる
ものだけど

You still have an imprudent mouth for a Shinigami.

But it is unsettling the other Shinigami more and more.

Tenshi wins Iku[edit]

Tenshi 地震の心配なら杞憂だよ
アレを仕掛けたからね 		No use worrying about earthquakes now,
I've put that in place, right?

Tenshi wins herself[edit]

Tenshi あー、勉強はめんどくさいなぁ
こんな昔の人の事ばっかり頭に叩き込んでもねぇ 		Ah, studying is such a chore...
Even if I learn all these things from the ancient people....

Tenshi wins someone[edit]

Tenshi 天人はたまに地上に降りて忠言するものよね
でーもー、私には到底実行できない忠言ばかりねぇ 		A celestial descends onto Earth to give advice.
Buuuut, for me they're all advice that can't be put to action.
