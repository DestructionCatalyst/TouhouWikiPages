Stage 1[edit]

掴もうぜ！オカルトボール

Seize the Occult Ball!

BGM: ボールのある日常

BGM: An Everyday Life with Balls

Marisa Kirisame ENTERS

Marisa

香霖堂に居たら
面白いもん見つけちゃった

この現象にはまだ誰も
気付いてないみたいだな

今のうちに
自分の力 (もの) にしてやるぜ！

I found somethin' real
interestin' here at Kourindou.

Looks like nobody's noticed
this phenomenon yet.

Better act now
an' take this power (thing) for my own!

恐怖！学校の魔法使い
霧雨　魔理沙

Horror! The School Magician
Marisa Kirisame

Reimu Hakurei ENTERS

Marisa

お、おう、どうした？
こんな時間に

O-oh, uh, hi. What're ya up to
at this hour?

Reimu

ちょっと面白い物
見つけたもんでねー

魔理沙 (あんた) 相手に試そうかなと

Well, I found something
kind of interesting...

...and figured I could test it out on Marisa (you) .

Marisa

試そう、ったあ
穏やかな話じゃ無いな

だがな、私もだぜ
丁度良いと思ってたんだ

遠慮無く試させて貰うぜ！
この不思議 (オカルト) な力を！

"Test it out," huh?
That doesn't sound too friendly.

But I've got somethin', too!
Ya came at just the right time.

Time to cut loose an' test this out on ya!
Behold this mysterious (occult) power!

BGM: オカルトアラカルト

BGM: Occult à la Carte

スペルカードとグレイズ

ダッシュや急上昇、急降下には触れた弾幕を避けたりかき消す
「グレイズ」という効果がついているぞ

強力な敵スペルカードも突っ込む事で活路が開く
弾幕にダッシュで飛びこんでみよう！

Spell Cards and Grazing

When dashing or leaping up & down, you'll erase bullets you touch.
This effect is called "grazing"!

You can open paths through strong enemy Spell Cards by dashing in.
Leap into the danmaku with a good, strong dash!

Reimu

あんたに試そう、ってのは
毒味 (きのこ) の事だったんだけど…

いきなり攻撃してくるなんて
何を見せてくれるのかしら？
まさかもうお終いじゃ無いわよね？

When I said 'test out', I actually meant
having you be my guinea pig (taste these mushrooms) ...

Just what are you trying to show me
by attacking me all of a sudden?
Don't tell me you're already finished, are you?

Marisa

当たり前だ

味わわせてやるぜ！
都市伝説 (オカルト) の新しい使い方をな！

‛Course not!

Have a taste a' this!
A new way to use the Urban Legends (occult) !

怪スペルとオカルトボール

オカルトボールが出現すると、敵は「怪スペル」を繰り出してくる

スペルカードより攻撃が激しく
こちらの攻撃も殆ど効かない反則状態、これは困った

狙うべき弱点は「オカルトボール」

ボールに接近し続ければ奪うことができ
「怪スペル」を阻止できるぞ！

Occult Spells and Occult Balls

When an Occult Ball appears, the enemy's using a "Weird Spell".

This is a foul-play type situation; an attack that's more ferocious than a Spell Card, and your attacks barely have any effect. Yikes!

The weak point you should aim for is the "Occult Ball".

By being in continual contact with the ball, you can steal it
and put a stop to the Weird Spell!

究極奥義・怪ラストワード

オカルトボールを４つ以上持っている時は
「必殺技＋スペルカードボタンの同時押し」で「怪ラストワード」が撃てるぞ
ボールを全て失うが、集めた数が多いほど威力アップ！

早速魔理沙に試し撃ちでも

Ultimate Arcanum: Weird Last Word

With 4 or more Occult Balls, you can use your "Weird Last Word"
by pressing both the Special Move and Spell Card buttons at once.
It uses all the Balls, but the more you have, the harder it hits!

Try hitting Marisa with it, quick.

If player loses

Marisa

これが、これが！
学校の七不思議だ！

Behold, behold!
These are the seven mysteries of the school!

Marisa Kirisame DEFEATED

Marisa

ま
そんなもんか

でも、面白くないか？
都市伝説は自由に操れるんだぜ？

Welp.
That's it, I guess.

Isn't it cool, though?
We can freely control the urban legends.

Reimu

わ、私も気が付いていたわよ

でも、さっきの
奇妙なボールは一体……？

W-well, I certainly realized that much.

But what was that
bizarre ball a second ago...?

Marisa

さあな

Beats me.

Stage 2[edit]

オカルトな仙人

An Occult Hermit

BGM: 顕現した伝承の形

BGM: Forms of Manifested Folklore

Reimu AND Kasen ENTER

Kasen

なるほどねぇ
魔理沙も、玉をねぇ

I see, I see.
So Marisa's also acquired a ball...

脅威！願いを訊く仙人
茨木　華扇

Menace! The Hermit Who Listens to Wishes
Kasen Ibaraki

Reimu

魔理沙、も？

Marisa, "also"?

Kasen

あ、うそうそごめん
何の話でしたっけ？

Oh, just kidding, sorry.
What were we talking about?

Reimu

ふん
とぼけたって無駄よ

あんたならあの都市伝説 (つくりばなし) について
何か知ってるかもと思ったけど
やっぱりね

Hmf.
It's no use playing dumb.

I thought you would know something
about the Urban Legends (make-believe) .
Looks like I was right.

Kasen

あの都市伝説って……
『七つのオカルトボールを
　　　　　　　集めると……』

That urban legend being...
"If you collect the
seven Occult Balls...

Kasen

Reimu

『願いが叶う』
　　　　って奴？

『大異変が起こる』
　　　　　　って奴

...your wish is granted,"
right?

"...a huge incident occurs,"
right.

Kasen

Reimu

え？

ん？

Eh?

Hm?

Reimu

まあいいや
でね、魔理沙が変な技使ったら
出てきたのよ、変な玉が

私はあの玉が
噂のオカルトボールだと思うのよねぇ

そもそも誰が都市伝説 (つくりばなし) を
利用して広めた噂なのか
判らないけど

Eh, whatever.
So when Marisa used this weird move,
this weird ball appeared too.

I would think that ball
was one of the rumored Occult Balls...

Although, in the first place, I dunno
if someone just spread that rumor by
as some kind of Urban Legend (make-believe) ...

Kasen

貴方の魔理沙の話が
本当だとすると

今オカルトボールを
持っているのは貴方

If your story about Marisa
is true, then...

You're the one holding that
Occult Ball right now.

Reimu

ん？

Hm?

Kasen

悪いけど……
今この場で頂くよ！

My apologies...
But I'll be taking that ball here and now!

BGM: 七玉蒐集ショウダウン

BGM: Seven-Orb Collection Showdown

If player loses

Kasen

オカルトボールは何故か実在する
霊夢が犯人とは思いがたいが……
でも危ないところだった……

These Occult Balls exist for unknown reasons.
It's hard to imagine Reimu being the culprit, but...
Well, anyway, that was a close one...

Kasen Ibaraki DEFEATED

Kasen

やっぱり
すんなり渡してはくれないか

ここで本気を出すのは
色々と危ないな
せめて警告だけでも

Well, I figured as much.
You're not going to hand it over that easily?

(Using my full strength here would be
risky for several reasons...
I ought to at least warn her, though.)

Reimu

何なのよ！
あんたも変な玉だして！

もしかして
本当に噂どおりに……？

What was that!?
You pulled out some weird ball, too!

Don't tell me it's just like
the rumor said...?

Kasen

霊夢、このボールは危ないわ
これは誰かが作った噂では無い

ボールだけ都市伝説 (つくりばなし) じゃ無いわ！
恐らく本物の大異変 (オカルト) よ！

Reimu, this ball is dangerous.
It isn't a rumor that someone fabricated.

This ball alone isn't some Urban Legend (make-believe) !
Most likely, this is legitimately A Great Incident (occult) !

深秘的 (オカルティズム) な夜の闇

Darkness of the Night of Occultism

Reimu

あいつったら変な事
言うもんだから

気になって眠れやしない

Geeze, thanks to all that
weird stuff she was saying...

I'm too intrigued to sleep.

???? ENTERS

Reimu

誰ッ？
こんな夜に

…………

反応が無い
つまり目の前には誰も居ない

なぁんだ、やっぱり私は寝てたのか
これは夢ね……

……ってそんな訳あるかい！
このオカルトオーラ！
やらねばやられるわ……！

Who's there?
On a night like this...?

...........

No reaction.
Meaning there's nobody in front of me.

Oh dear, I must be sleeping after all.
This is just a dream...

...or rather, not!
Not with this occult aura!
Better strike before they do...! [1]

ｔｏ　ｂｅ　ｃｏｎｔｉｎｕｅ．．．

to be continued...

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ On the C87 trial version, the scene finishes with Reimu's attack just almost reaching the opponent, while she is hit back on the web trial and official versions.
  • This prologue is the same as the story included in the trial version. This is also the only story that one can initially play as Reimu; her full story is unlocked later on.
